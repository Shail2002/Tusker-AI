Our Mission

Our TUSKER Founder had witnessed that organizations of all sizes recognize the tremendous potential of data, but many struggle to turn it into actionable insights that improve their business results. The conventional legacy approach slows organizations down, requires costly software and too many specific tools used by too many uniquely skilled people and that's where TUSKER AI Products walks in this scenario.


Our Product simplifies the computer vision complexity overhead and upgrades your business verticals with our User-Interface Automated Engine. With our assistance in data models now you can reduce complexity, making it easier to design, deploy and understand data sources to meet business needs. TUSKER AI not only automates and standardizes model design tasks but also includes complex queries so that you can improve business alignment by ensuring data integrity and simplifying integration. Our end-to-end Product converges three key core pillars of automation and transformation involving our pre-trained model – data, processes and people – to enable the democratization of data, automation of business processes and the upskilling of people for quick wins and transformative business outcomes. Users start with the business case and quickly create analytics, data science and process automation outcomes, without the requirements of a specialized skill set with our Automated Product. The result is faster insights and business impact, fully automated business processes and the ability to rapidly upskill. TUSKER product’s Deep Learning model has prebuilt recognition and prediction models to identify a range of concepts including - Objects, Incidents, Emotions and do Predictions, for industrial grade deployments.


TUSKER AI Products are powered for its efficiency, effectiveness and for accurate computer vision-based data platform aiding businesses with real-time intelligent analysis and automation known for project implementation, development and excellent customer satisfaction. The underlying motive of the company is to serve its consumers to the fullest ensuring 100% customer satisfaction. We aim to grow further building a wider area of the network.

TUSKER AI Product delivers end-to-end automation of Vision analytics, deep learning models, and enables the agility needed to accelerate Business verticals using our Automated AI-Engine..
